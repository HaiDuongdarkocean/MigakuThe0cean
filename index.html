Notes

The term "糊涂" refers to a state of being confused or muddled. When someone says they are "糊涂了," it indicates that they find themselves in a state of confusion, not clearly understanding a situation or feeling perplexed. The nuance is one of temporary befuddlement or puzzlement.The term `糊涂` refers to a state of confusion or muddled understanding. It describes when someone is unclear, bewildered, or has difficulty grasping something. It can also imply that a person is not very alert or sharp at the moment. In the context provided, the nuance suggests that the speaker is somewhat perplexed or puzzled.
